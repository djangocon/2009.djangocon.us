Welcome to DjangoCon '09

0 Comments | Leave a Comment

  • Tags: djangocon09 |
  • Posted: 06 July, 2009

We’re back and this time we’ll be at the DoubleTree Green hotel in Portland Oregon from September 8th to September 12th. The first 3 days are conference days and the last 2 days are sprint days.

If you would like to purchase tickets you can get them from http://djangocon09.eventbrite.com. The earlybird tickets are currently on sale.

We’ll keep adding information to the site and the wiki. If you would like to volunteer to help out at DjangoCon this year, please put your name on our wiki at djangocon.pbworks.com.

Hope to see you all there!

Rob
