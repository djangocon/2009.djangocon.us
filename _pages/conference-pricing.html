Pricing

These are the prices for DjangoCon '09. If you're paying for yourself, make sure register at the hobbyist rate. If your company is paying for you to go then please register at the corporate rate, it helps us to make keep things affordable and offer financial aid to those that need it. Please ensure you only sign up for student tickets if you have a way of proving your full-time student status.

All prices include lunch and a coffee break in the afternoon.

Early-bird Prices (6th - 19th July 2009)

  • Corporate: $400
  • Hobbyist: $210
  • Student: $135

Regular Prices (20th July - 1st September)

  • Corporate: $480
  • Hobbyist: $290
  • Student: $200
