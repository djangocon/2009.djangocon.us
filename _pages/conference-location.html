Location

Summary

DjangoCon '09 will be held at the DoubleTree Green Hotel in Portland, Oregon.

The Doubletree Hotel Portland is proud to be one of the largest hotels in Oregon. The hotel offers 476 guestrooms, 38,000 square feet of meeting space and holds an amazing 8,500 square foot Executive Meeting Center approved by International Association of Conference Centers (IACC).

The hotel is located adjacent to the MAX light rail line which allows easy access from the Airport and into the heart of downtown Portland. It is also located across the street from Oregon's largest shopping mall, Lloyd Center and is 4 easy walkable blocks to the Oregon Convention Center.

Location

Address: Doubletree Hotel & Executive Meeting Center, Portland, Lloyd Center 1000 NE Multnomah Street Portland, OR 97232

Rates

Both single and double occupancy rooms will be: $99.

In order to book at this rate please wait for an email after you have registered that will contain a link to a booking form. Do not try and book rooms directly with the hotel.

Directions

From the airport via MAX Light Rail: Board MAX Red Line to City Center & Beaverton TC. Get off at Lloyd Center/NE 11th Ave. MAX Station. Walk half a block to the hotel. Cost $2.05 - Trimet

From the airport: Merge on I-205 South. Take the I-84 West exit toward Portland City Center. Take Exit 1, Lloyd Center (Lloyd Blvd). Turn right at the third signal (9th Ave). The hotel is one block up on the left.
