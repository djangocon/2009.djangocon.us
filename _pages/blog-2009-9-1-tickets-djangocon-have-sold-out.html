Tickets for DjangoCon have Sold Out

0 Comments | Leave a Comment

  • Tags: djangocon09, tickets |
  • Posted: 01 September, 2009

Tickets for this years DjangoCon have sold out. If you would like to be put on a waiting list in case anyone cancels please mail me via the contact form in the contact section.

For those wondering, we always have a cap in place to make it easier to budget. It looked like this year would be a difficult year for conferences, so we budgeted for a lower amount. Next year we might increase the attendance level.

To help some of you in future I'll put a notification system in place so that if there are fewer than x amount of tickets you will be mailed to let you know to hurry up. There will be more details on that after DjangoCon '09.
